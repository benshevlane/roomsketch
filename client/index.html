How it works

  1. Draw your walls — Click to place points and connect them. Walls snap to a 10cm grid automatically.
  2. Add furniture — Drag sofas, units, appliances, and doors from the library. Resize to your exact dimensions.
  3. Export and send — Download a clean PNG and send it to your fitter, supplier, or architect. They don't need an account.

Room-specific planning tools

  • Kitchen Planner — Plan your kitchen layout with units, appliances, and islands.
  • Bathroom Planner — Position sanitaryware and fittings before the plumber arrives.
  • Bedroom Planner — Find the best bed position and storage configuration.
  • Living Room Planner — Arrange sofas, tables, and TV units to find the perfect layout.
  • Floor Plan Maker — Create detailed multi-room floor plans for renovations and new builds.

Features

  • Snap-to-grid — every wall snaps to a 10cm grid so the dimensions you send to a supplier are actually accurate
  • Live measurements — wall lengths update in real time as you draw, in metres, centimetres, or feet
  • 30+ furniture items — sofas, beds, kitchen units, doors, windows — drag from the library and resize to fit
  • Export as image — download a clean PNG and send it to your fitter, kitchen maker, or architect
  • Room labels — name each space so your plan reads clearly at a glance
  • Undo / redo — made a mistake? Ctrl+Z takes you back

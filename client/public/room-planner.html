How it works

From blank page to shared plan in three steps

Free Room Planner is built for homeowners, not architects. No training required.

1

Draw your walls

Click to place wall points and chain them together. Alignment guides keep everything straight. Walls snap to 90° by default and display live measurements.

2

Add furniture

Drag sofas, beds, tables, doors, and windows from the library. Resize and rotate to fit. The canvas pans as you draw so you never run out of room.

3

Export and share

Download a PNG floor plan — watermarked with freeroomplanner.com — and share it with whoever needs to see it. Or save as JSON to reload your plan later.

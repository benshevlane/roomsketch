How it works

From blank canvas to complete floor plan in three steps

Free Room Planner is built for homeowners and property professionals. No CAD skills required.

1

Measure and draw

Measure your rooms and draw walls to scale. Chain wall segments together, add interior walls to divide spaces. Snap-to-grid keeps everything accurate.

2

Add doors, windows & furniture

Place doors with swing arcs, mark window positions, then drag furniture from the library to complete each room.

3

Export and share

Download a PNG floor plan branded with freeroomplanner.com and share it with builders, architects, estate agents, or anyone who needs it.

Features

Everything you need for floor plans

Purpose-built for creating clear, accurate floor plans.

Snap-to-grid accuracy
Every wall snaps to a 10 cm grid so your dimensions are accurate without measuring twice.
Live measurements
Wall lengths update in real time as you draw — in metres or feet.
Multi-room layouts
Draw interior walls to divide a floor into separate rooms. Each room can have its own label.
Doors & windows
Add door swings and window openings to make your floor plan readable at a glance.
30+ furniture items
Sofas, beds, tables, kitchen units — drag and drop from the library to furnish each room.
Room labels
Name each space so your plan reads clearly — kitchen, lounge, en-suite.
Room area calculation
Automatic floor area calculation for each enclosed room — useful for flooring and tiling estimates.
Export as PNG
Download a clean floor plan image to share with whoever needs it.
No account required
Open the link and start drawing. No email, no sign-up, no friction.
